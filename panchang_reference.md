### Notes on Astronomical Data

The following values require actual astronomical calculation for precise accuracy:
- **Thithi** (திதி): Lunar day - transitions based on Moon's position
- **Star** (நட்சத்திரம்): Nakshatra - based on Moon's position in the zodiac
- **Yogam** (யோகம்): Combination of Sun and Moon longitudes
- **Lagnam** (லக்னம்): Rising sign at the time of sunrise

For production use, these should be calculated using:
1. Swiss Ephemeris or similar astronomical library
2. Location-specific sunrise/sunset times
3. Ayanamsa correction for sidereal calculations
